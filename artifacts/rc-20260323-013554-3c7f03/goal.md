# Research Goal

**SMART Goal:** Conduct a systematic literature review of published empirical studies examining the effects of patient-provider racial and ethnic concordance on communication quality, diagnostic accuracy, treatment adherence, and health outcomes among Black patients in the United States, synthesizing evidence from at least 25 peer-reviewed sources to identify consistent patterns, mechanisms, and gaps in the current evidence base, with the final deliverable being a publication-ready review manuscript with verified references.

**Domains:** Health disparities, medical sociology, primary care, patient-provider communication

**Mode:** docs-first (literature review — no experiment execution required)
