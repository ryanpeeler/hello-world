# Problem Decomposition

## Central Question
What are the effects of patient-provider racial and ethnic concordance on healthcare quality and outcomes for Black patients in the United States?

## Sub-Questions

### 1. Communication Quality
- Does racial concordance affect visit duration, patient-centered communication, and information exchange?
- What communication mechanisms mediate concordance effects?
- How do shared cultural frameworks influence patient-provider interaction?

### 2. Diagnostic Accuracy and Clinical Decision-Making
- Does implicit racial bias affect diagnostic accuracy for Black patients?
- Does concordance reduce bias-related diagnostic errors?
- What role does concordance play in pain assessment and treatment decisions?

### 3. Treatment Adherence
- Does concordance improve medication adherence across disease categories?
- Are concordance effects on adherence disease-specific or generalizable?
- Does concordance affect treatment initiation timing?

### 4. Health Outcomes
- What is the relationship between concordance and mortality?
- Does concordance affect preventive service utilization?
- How do patient satisfaction and trust mediate concordance-outcome relationships?

### 5. Mechanisms and Moderators
- What are the primary causal mechanisms (trust, bias reduction, communication)?
- What factors moderate concordance effects (disease severity, setting, SES)?
- How does historical medical mistrust shape concordance dynamics?

### 6. Policy Implications
- What workforce diversity interventions are supported by the evidence?
- Can cultural competency training substitute for concordance effects?
- What structural interventions can reduce concordance-dependent disparities?
